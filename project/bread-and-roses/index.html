Bread and Roses

In 2017 I created a series of pictures about acknowledgement and empathy. Each picture is a little story about a different normal, some happy, some real, and some just for fun.

This body of work came together randomly, in between other projects, and was shown in Portland from time to time until 2020 when we all put everything away and went home.
