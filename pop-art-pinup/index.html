Pop Art Pinup

For several years I published an annual calendar featuring classic pinup pictures. It all began when I took on the job of catalog photography for a pinup clothing designer in Portland. My studio was in her warehouse, which gave us access to some of the finest new and vintage fashions available. Pop Art Pinup became a lifestyle brand, and later grew into Mouse and Moon Creations, which saw me return to fairy tale themed stories and pictures on a much larger scale than a calendar. For a time, however, it was all very mid-century modern with a wink to contemporary style.
