Bread and Roses

In 2017 I created a series of pictures about acknowledgement and empathy, and how the world looks and feels through the eyes of someone with borderline personality disorder. Each picture is a little story about a different normal, some happy, some real, and some just for fun. These are some of my favorites. Find in them what you will.
