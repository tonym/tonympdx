Bread and Roses

In 2017 I created a series of pictures about acknowledgement and empathy, and how the world looks and feels through the eyes of borderline personality disorder. I have BPD. Through art I learn more about myself, about how I see others, and about how they see me. Each picture is a little story about a different normal, some happy, some real, and some just for fun. These are some of my favorites. Find in them what you will.
