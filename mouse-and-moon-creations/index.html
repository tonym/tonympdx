Mouse and Moon Creations

Mouse and Moon began as a book project, steampunk fairy tales, but soon became an operating studio, offering creative services to commercial clients. The original Mouse and Moon pictures were made over a 3 year period, and shown at various galleries and conventions around Portland. For the most part, the sets, props, costumes and jewelry were made either in house or by local artisans. These are just a few of the many pictures I made for this project.
