About

I'm Tony M. I'm an artist living and working in Portland, Oregon. I also design and build websites and user interfaces. I began by studying creative writing at Boise State University, built my first website in 1997 as a way to share my stories and poems, and have been creating both for the web and for print ever since. Everything here is a confluence of creative energy and digital knowledge.

Artist's statment

Capturing light is the beginning, not the end. A picture might start as a photo, but the result is a series of choices on the path to the final product. My medium is digital, manipulative, and obvious, but I know what every picture will look like before I reach for the camera or computer. My subjects range from fairy tales to cultural comment, all with a positive, whimsical approach. Even the serious stuff should make you smile, just a little.

Designer's statment

I build design systems. Often we think of content as a factor of design, but design also lives in, and serves the presentation of the content. Whether it be branding, art, commerce or blog, the system is what wraps content in presentation, and offers a consistent user experience. This is no different than the layout of a magazine, or the arrangement of art in a gallery, the surroundings compliment and present each piece, and each piece is part of the presentation. My creative work is about making content, my professional work is about presenting it.
